About QuirkyCart

Welcome to QuirkyCart, your number one source for all things shopping. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2022 by Parul Vats, Quirkycart has come a long way from its beginnings in a small office. When i first started out, my passion for eco-friendly products drove me to this passion, and gave me the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
